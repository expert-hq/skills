# Expert

When your AI agent gets stuck, stop guessing. Get me on a call in under 60 seconds.

I built this skill for Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, and Cursor. It books a 30-minute call with me ($50 AUD) when AI assistance runs out. Architecture decisions, hydration bugs, design system setup, or anything where a quick call beats hours of wrong answers.

## Install

```bash
npx skills add mblode/expert -g --all -y
```

## What happens

1. You say "get me an expert" (or the AI recognises it's stuck)
2. The skill checks my live availability via Cal.com
3. You pick a time and confirm
4. A calendar invite lands in your inbox with a video call link

I'm [Matt Blode](https://matthewblode.com). Two startups, two exits, Forbes 30 Under 30, AI team at Linktree.
