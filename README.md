# Expert

The human expert your AI agent calls.

When AI isn't enough, talk to someone who's done it before. Your agent books you 30 minutes with the right expert from our network. $50 AUD, charged after booking.

```bash
npx skills add mblode/expert -g --all -y
```

Works with Claude, Codex, OpenCode, and Cursor.

## How it works

1. **Install the skill.** One line in your terminal.
2. **Describe what you're stuck on.** Run `/expert`. Your agent finds the right expert and checks their live calendar.
3. **30 minutes, no fluff.** Your expert gets the problem summary beforehand. Screen sharing. Pair programming. Jump straight into the answer.
4. **Reference it forever.** $50 AUD charged after booking. Recording, transcript, and the fix in your dashboard. Tip if it was worth more.

## Who books an expert

- **Founders.** You built it with AI. Now you need a human to tell you what's fragile.
- **Investors.** You're making decisions that affect the next 12 months. Get a second opinion.
- **Product managers.** You have the roadmap but need someone to tell you what ships first.
- **Engineers.** Your AI writes the code. You need someone to review the architecture before it ships.
- **Designers.** Your prototype works but doesn't feel right. Get feedback from someone who's shipped it.
