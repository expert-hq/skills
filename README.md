# Expert

When your AI agent gets stuck, stop guessing. Get a human on a call in under 60 seconds.

This skill teaches Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, and Cursor to book a 30-minute expert call ($50 AUD) when AI assistance runs out — architecture decisions, hydration bugs, design system setup, or anything where a quick call beats hours of wrong answers.

## Install

```bash
npx skills add mblode/expert -g --all -y
```

## What happens

1. You say "get me an expert" (or the AI recognises it's stuck)
2. The skill checks live availability via Cal.com
3. You pick a time and confirm
4. A calendar invite lands in your inbox with a video call link

The expert is [Matt Blode](https://matthewblode.com) — two startups, two exits, Forbes 30 Under 30, AI team at Linktree.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|-------|--------------|
| `expert` | Check availability, present slots, and book a 30-minute call ($50 AUD) with a human expert |
